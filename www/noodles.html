Time Taken: 5 Mins

Serves: 1 Person

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet of Koka Noodles
  • 100ml Boiling Hot Water
  • 1 Flavour Packet

Steps Involved

Step 1: Place Noodles in Bowl

Step 2: Boil Kettle and Add to Noodles

Step 3: Add a Plate ontop to Seal in Heat

Step 4: Wait 2 mins

Step 5: Drain out Water !Carefully!

Step 6: Add Flavour Packet

Step 7: Enjoy Yo Noodles!
