Time Taken: 60 mins

Serves: 2 - 3 People

Ingredients

  • 500g of Mince Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Creamy Sauce
  • Optional: Bacon Strips
  • Onion Slices
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Garlic
  • Cheese

Steps Involved

Step 1: Add Mince Meat to Frying Pan

Step 2: Cook until Brown

Step 3: Add in Tomato Sauce                                            (Lower Temperature)

Step 4: Cook Bacon and Onion Slices                                  (Separate Frying Pan)

Step 5: Add Bacon and Onion to Mince

Step 6: Add Mince to Oven Dish

Step 7: Add Layer of Creamy Sauce

Step 8: Cover with Pasta Sheets

Step 9: Repeat Steps (6 - 8)

Step 10: Sprinkle top layer with Cheese

Step 11: Cook in Pre Heated Oven for 30 mins (220C)

Step 12: Cool Down & Enjoy
